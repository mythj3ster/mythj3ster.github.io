About Me

My name is Anthony Darren Sutanto. I was born in the United States, but I was raised in Indonesia. I have been taking pictures since I was little. From my Mom's or Dad's Blackberry phone, Iphone 4s, to my Dad's first DSLR Camera...the Canon 1000D. Now, I have my own camera, the Canon 6D and it has been my favorite and my go to DSLR. I hope and wish I could continue this journey, to capture moments and the beauty of nature. Enjoy my works :)
